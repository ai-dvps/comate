---
title: Download and install
step: 1
---

Visit [GitHub Releases](https://github.com/ai-dvps/comate/releases) and download the installer for your platform:

- **macOS**: Download the `.dmg` and drag Comate into your Applications folder.
- **Windows**: Download the `.msi` and follow the setup wizard.

On first launch, your system may ask you to confirm the developer — please allow it to proceed.
